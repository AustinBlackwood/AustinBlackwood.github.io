Austin Blackwood Likes To Make Things Interesting

Everyday as an IT Tech I have discussions in situations where one party does not understand anything about a given subject. This requires a patient attitude, and a mind for analogical thought. Finding things we both understand, and using that as an interface to translate solutions, but also realizing that they do not NEED to understand every detail. This is the personification of writing logic; having a conversation through an interface where you know next to nothing about the user. The tools we use are just a way to achieve that goal. It's pretty fun too.
